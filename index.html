Welcome, Reader

“The world you know has ended. The story begins.”

Discover More

Omniscient Reader’s Viewpoint (ORV) is a South Korean web novel written by Sing Shong that follows the story of Kim Dokja, an ordinary office worker whose favorite web novel suddenly becomes reality. What makes Omniscient Reader’s Viewpoint truly captivating is its exploration of storytelling, fate, and human connection. Beyond its thrilling action and intricate world-building, the novel dives into themes of empathy, sacrifice, and what it means to exist within or beyond a story.

As the only person who knows the entire story’s plot, Kim Dokja must use his knowledge to survive in a newly transformed world filled with deadly scenarios, gods, and mythical beings. Blending fantasy, psychological depth, and survival, ORV explores themes of fate, storytelling, and the power of human connection as Dokja and his companions fight to change a story that was once thought to be unchangeable.

Main Characters

Kim Dokja

The main protagonist of the web novel, manhwa, and
upcoming movie Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint. He is
a solitary office worker and the sole reader of a
webnovel called Three Ways to Survive in a Ruined
World. His life becomes reality when the events of
the novel begin to unfold in the real world.

Yoo Jonghyuk

A determined regressor who has lived through many timelines.
Also known as the strongest regressor who has lived through
the story countless times. He has a cold and serious personality,
shaped by the pain of repeating the same world over and over.
Despite seeming distant, he deeply cares about others, even if
he rarely shows it directly.

Han Sooyoung

Yoo Han Sooyoung is a witty and sharp-tongued writer in
Omniscient Reader’s Viewpoint, known for her bold personality
and playful teasing. She hides her vulnerability behind humor,
but she genuinely treasures the people close to her. Even in
dangerous situations, she stays clever and confident, always
thinking several steps ahead.

Jung Huiwon

A marginalized survivor who becomes a vital member of
Kim Dokja’s party. She is fiercely committed to her own sense
of justice and serves as the “sword” of her group, wielding great
power to protect the weak and confront evil. Over the course of
the story, she wrestles with her trauma, loyalty, and the moral
complexities of justice.

Yoo Sangah

Yoo Sangah is introduced as a colleague of the protagonist
Kim Dokja at Mino Soft and becomes entwined in the sudden shift where
their world mirrors the novel Three Ways to Survive in a Ruined World.
She is depicted as gentle and lively but harbors a strong sense of self
and moral integrity, refusing to compromise her principles even
under extreme pressure.

Lee Hyunsung

A former soldier who becomes one of Kim Dokja’s most loyal
companions in Omniscient Reader’s Viewpoint. Despite
his tough appearance, he’s gentle-hearted, values protecting
others, and struggles with guilt and responsibility, making him
one of the most emotionally grounded characters in the story.

Bihyung

The dokkaebi who initially hosts Kim Dokja’s scenario,
acting like a mischievous game master observing the
events. Though he appears playful and greedy for
entertainment, he gradually develops a strange fondness
and respect for Kim Dokja and his group.
